About Me

Festival-recognized Director of Photography with over 14 years of experience across feature films and OTT productions in Tamil, Malayalam, Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, and Punjabi. Known for crafting emotionally grounded visual narratives supported by strong technical expertise in digital color science, HDR mastering, and multi-format delivery workflows.

Cinematic Style & Approach: "I approach cinematography as emotional architecture. Light is not used merely to illuminate, but to shape psychological space. My visual language is grounded in realism yet responsive to narrative tension. Whether working within controlled LED volume environments or natural night exteriors, I design frames that serve performance first."
